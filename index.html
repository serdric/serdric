Welcome to the future

Fantasy texts are a subgenre of fiction. Often they transport readers to far off land, a time in the future, or a place where unique creatures live. We read these texts to escape our lives and follow the journey of a hero.

This year we will be following our hero into the wardrobe to a far off land called Narnia.

Joseph Campbell's Hero's Journey

Many fantasy texts follow a simple algorithm. This algorithm was coined by Jospeh Campbell as a, "Hero's Journey". Stay tunned to find out more about what this means.

Timeline

Fantasy texts exist in two timelines. One is the ordinary word, while the other is the supernatural world.

The ordinary world is the status quo. What we know as regular life.

The supernatural world is the place where our hero will visit and take place in adventures.
